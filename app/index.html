Mixin

Mobile App UI/UX Design

navigate_next

Mixin is a self-initiated project I started to fulfill my passion for music. This hypothetical project is how I would design a social music app. I use Box to conveniently cloud-host my music. Mixin combines that insight with a fresh UI. Innovative UX capabilities would increase one's abilities to share, navigate, and manipulate content such as mixes, playlists, and moods. Global metrics would uniquely show how many people own, share, and are currently listening to songs or artists. Born out of a deep love for modern design and music, Mixin is an opportunity for me to exhibit and practice a variety of design skills.

UI/UX Skills   • concept + prototyping   • interaction design   • motion design
